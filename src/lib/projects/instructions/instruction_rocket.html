1. In order to start the animation, move the slider from leftmost position, and press the Start Button. Press Stop to Stop/Pause the animation.
  2. You Can Add Blue Orbs on the screen by pressing "Add Orb" button, and clear them all by pressing "Clear Orbs".
  3. Press A and D keys to smoothly turn the ship left or right.
  4. Press "Change Direction" to randomly change the direction of velocity of the ship.
  5. Press the button that has a "Change Background" label, to change the background color (Dynamic option changes the background with the animation slider).
  6. Press the button that has a "Change ship color" label.
