I decided to simulate the same phenomena on the web, by making the browser record the number collisions and perform the calculations of velocities of both objects after their ellastic collision. The browser computes velocities from these two formulas:

v A f = ( m A − m B m A + m B ) v A i + ( 2 m B m A + m B ) v B i v B f = ( 2 m A m A + m B ) v A i + ( m B − m A m A + m B ) v B i
