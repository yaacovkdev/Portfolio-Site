When you come together for a hackathon, you often feel excitement, a sense of wander, and perhaps even attempts to formulate plans for tasks you aren't sure you will be doing. When I attended #HACKVAN 2025, I was excited to use my talent to solve a problem from a Christian perspective. Our team decided to build a full-stack Chatbot application that would utilize training data to provide users with awareness of their financial habits. My involvement in the hackathon was building the API and monitoring user testing.
